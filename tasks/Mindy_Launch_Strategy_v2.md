## **The TikTok question (and why we treat it carefully)**

FAR 52.204-27 prohibits TikTok on any device used for federal contract performance. That does not stop us from posting on TikTok — we are a media company, not a contractor working a task order. But it does mean our highest-LTV buyers cannot use TikTok on work devices, and many have removed it entirely. Practical implication: TikTok is discovery, Reels is conversion.

| Platform priority — in this order 1\.  Instagram Reels — Eric’s strongest audience (11K @ecoffie) and where our buyer actually checks. This is also where @meetmindy lives. 2\.  YouTube Shorts — feeds the 49K-subscriber channel and the existing podcast funnel. 3\.  LinkedIn — federal buyers, prime contractors, teaming partners. Mindy posts here in a more analyst voice. 4\.  TikTok — discovery and top-of-funnel awareness. Same content, lower conversion expectation. 5\.  X — recycled hooks \+ threads. Cheap to run, occasionally lands with the policy crowd. |
