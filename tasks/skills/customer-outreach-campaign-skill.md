# Customer Outreach Campaign Skill

**Purpose:** Turn a qualified customer list into outreach that produces calls, product insight, upgrades, proof stories, and white-glove opportunities.

This skill is for Annelle, Sikander, Eric, or any team member running customer-first outreach for MI.

## When To Use

Use this skill when:

- Annelle/Sikander need a larger qualified list.
- Recent purchasers need onboarding or feedback.
- MI Pro users need interviews.
- MI Free users need activation nudges.
- Bundle buyers need account setup help.
- 10-10 Forever candidates need founder outreach.
- A launch, bootcamp, or new feature needs customer conversations.

## Campaign Principle

Talk to users first. Advisory second.

The goal is not to convince people. The goal is to find the committed customers already showing signal and help them get results.

## Inputs

- Ranked customer list
- Segment labels: MI Free, MI Pro, bundle, internal, white-glove, audience only
- Purchase history
- Activation state
- Profile state
- Email/app engagement
- Current offer or reason for outreach
- Owner list

## Segment-Specific Goals

| Segment | Goal | Primary Ask |
| --- | --- | --- |
| MI Free | Complete profile and open first useful alert | Setup help |
| MI Pro | Understand value, improve retention, identify outcomes | 20-minute product call |
| Bundle / Ultimate | Make sure access is working and consolidate into MI | Account setup call |
| Active high-value user | Learn what makes MI valuable | 10-10 customer call |
| White-glove candidate | Qualify for execution help | Strategy call |
| Dormant free user | Light reactivation only | Profile completion nudge |

## Outreach Rules

- Keep the first message short.
- Lead with observed context, not generic marketing.
- Ask for help improving MI, not just a sale.
- Do not pitch white-glove until the user gives execution pain.
- Do not send MI Free alerts to MI Pro users if Pro sends already cover the need.
- Always log outcome and next step.

## Message Templates

### MI Pro Interview

Subject: Quick question about your Market Intelligence access

Hi {{first_name}},

I saw you have access to Market Intelligence and wanted to ask for 20 minutes of feedback while we improve the product.

The main thing we are trying to learn is whether MI is helping you find better federal opportunities and decide what is worth pursuing.

Would you be open to a short call this week?

### Bundle / Ultimate Setup

Subject: Making sure your MI access is working

Hi {{first_name}},

You should have Market Intelligence access through your purchase. We are cleaning up setup and want to make sure your account, profile, alerts, and briefings are working correctly.

Can we help you get your profile configured so the opportunities match your business?

### MI Free Activation

Subject: Your federal opportunity alerts need one quick setup step

Hi {{first_name}},

You have access to the free Market Intelligence alerts, but the quality depends on your business profile.

If you add your NAICS codes and service area, the system can send better matches instead of generic opportunities.

### Founder / 10-10 Candidate

Subject: I would value your input on where GovCon Giants is going

Hi {{first_name}},

I am restructuring GovCon Giants around Market Intelligence and execution support instead of training alone.

You are one of the people whose behavior and background make me want to ask directly: what would make this platform truly valuable enough to use every week?

Would you have 30 minutes for a candid conversation?

## Call Script

1. Confirm what they bought or accessed.
2. Ask what kind of contracts they are trying to find.
3. Ask the two Wealth GENIUS questions.
4. Ask what they expected MI to do.
5. Ask what has been useful or confusing.
6. Ask what would make them use MI weekly.
7. Ask what would make them upgrade, stay, or refer.
8. Log the outcome.

## Follow-Up Rules

| Outcome | Follow-Up |
| --- | --- |
| Setup needed | Send setup link and offer help |
| Match quality issue | Ask for NAICS, keywords, geography, exclusions |
| Wants team workflow | Show Team Access / Deal Flow Board path |
| Wants white-glove | Route to Eric |
| Case study candidate | Ask permission and collect result details |
| Upgrade candidate | Send MI Pro offer |
| Not now | Put into light nurture |

## Metrics

Track campaign quality by:

- Contacted
- Replies
- Calls booked
- Calls completed
- Profiles completed after outreach
- MI logins after outreach
- Upgrades
- White-glove opportunities
- Product insights
- Proof stories

## Done Criteria

The campaign is complete when:

- Every qualified contact has an owner and next step.
- Outcomes are logged.
- Product insights are summarized.
- Upgrade and white-glove candidates are separated.
- The next batch is ranked from real behavior, not guesswork.
