Let's be honest about why small contractors bid less than they should: the proposal grind.

A solicitation lands. It's long. Somewhere in there are the requirements, the evaluation factors, the compliance items you can't miss. Reading it carefully eats a day. The compliance matrix eats another. Most small teams just… don't bid.

But the math is brutal: you can't win the contracts you don't pursue. Every solicitation you skip because it's too much work is a competitor's win by default.

The fix isn't working harder. It's cutting the grind — pull the requirements fast, get a compliance matrix you can trust, start from a first draft instead of a blank page.

Your homework: stop letting page count decide your pipeline. The next solicitation that fits — pursue it, even if it looks heavy.
