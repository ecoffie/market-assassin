## The Opportunity

### What is APEX?

APEX Accelerators (formerly PTACs) are federally-funded centers that help small businesses win government contracts. They provide free counseling, training, and support.

**Scale:**
- 90+ locations nationwide
- 300+ counselors
- 30,000+ small businesses served annually
- Funded by DoD + state governments

**Their clients = Our target customer.**

### Why This Matters

Every small business that walks into an APEX office needs exactly what MI provides:
- Daily opportunity alerts
- Market intelligence
- Recompete tracking
- Agency spending data
- Contractor database for teaming

APEX counselors are already teaching these businesses to use SAM.gov, USASpending, etc. — the 11 platforms we've unified.

## Positioning Options

### Option A: Sell to APEX (B2B)

**Model:** APEX buys MI licenses for their clients

| Package | What APEX Gets | Price |
|---------|----------------|-------|
| **Site License** | MI access for all clients at one location | $2,500/mo |
| **State License** | MI access for all locations in state | $10,000/mo |
| **National License** | APEX-wide deployment | Custom |

**Pros:**
- Large contract value
- Recurring revenue
- Built-in distribution

**Cons:**
- Long sales cycle (government-funded orgs)
- Complex procurement
- Need to prove ROI to funders

### Option B: Partner with APEX (Referral)

**Model:** APEX recommends MI to their clients, we pay referral fee

| Structure | How It Works |
|-----------|--------------|
| Free tier for APEX clients | Anyone with APEX referral gets MI Free + 30-day Pro trial |
| Revenue share | APEX gets % of conversions (10-20%) |
| Co-branded training | Joint bootcamps, APEX promotes, we deliver |

**Pros:**
- Lower friction
- APEX has no cost
- We own the customer relationship

**Cons:**
- Lower per-customer value
- Need APEX buy-in without payment

### Option C: APEX Clients Direct (B2C)

**Model:** Market directly to small businesses in APEX ecosystem

| Channel | How It Works |
|---------|--------------|
| APEX event sponsorship | Sponsor APEX conferences, get attendee lists |
| APEX graduate targeting | Target businesses that completed APEX programs |
| Counselor referral program | Individual counselors earn referral bonuses |

**Pros:**
- Direct customer relationship
- No complex B2B sale
- Faster revenue

**Cons:**
- Need to identify APEX clients
- Competing for attention
