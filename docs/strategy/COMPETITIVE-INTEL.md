## Competitive Positioning by Tool

### Market Assassin vs GovWin IQ
- **Their strength:** 150 analysts, pre-RFP intel years out
- **Their weakness:** $29K/yr, complex, enterprise-only
- **Our angle:** "Enterprise intel at SMB prices. 60 seconds, not 60 hours."

### Opportunity Hunter vs GovTribe
- **Their strength:** SAM.gov tracking, saved alerts, integrations
- **Their weakness:** $60-300/mo, no free tier, learning curve
- **Our angle:** "Free tier to start. Pro for $49 one-time. No monthly ransom."

### Recompete Tracker vs HigherGov
- **Their strength:** Best teaming partner finder, subcontract tracking
- **Their weakness:** Less known, limited market presence
- **Our angle:** "Expiring contracts + incumbent intel + win probability"

### Daily Briefings vs All
- **Their model:** Manual saved searches, email alerts, analyst reports
- **Our angle:** "AI reads everything. You get the 10 things that matter. Every morning."
