### Before Each Call

1. Open the tracking spreadsheet (create one or use Google Sheet)
2. Have the person's email ready
3. Have the two questions written in front of you
4. Have a place to write exact quotes

### Call Script (Follow Exactly)

**Opening (30 seconds):**

```
"Hi [NAME], this is Sikander from GovCon Giants. Thanks for taking the time!

Eric asked me to get your feedback — it's going to directly shape what we build next. I just have 2 quick questions, and I want to hear your honest thoughts. Ready?"
```

**Question 1:**

```
"What is the ONE thing about GovCon Giants—if we took it away—you would NOT want to work with us anymore?"
```

- **Let them talk.** Don't interrupt.
- **Write down their exact words.** Don't paraphrase.
- **If they give multiple things, ask:** "If you had to pick just ONE, which would it be?"

**Transition:**

```
"Great, that's really helpful. Second question..."
```

**Question 2:**

```
"What is the ONE thing you wish we added that would make you stay forever?"
```

- **Let them talk.** Don't interrupt.
- **Write down their exact words.** Don't paraphrase.
- **If they're vague, probe:** "Can you tell me more about what that would look like?"

**Closing (30 seconds):**

```
"This is exactly what Eric needs to hear. Thank you so much for your time — your feedback is going directly into what we build next. Have a great day!"
```

### What to Listen For (Don't Lead, Just Note)

| If they say... | It validates... |
|----------------|-----------------|
| "Daily alerts" / "briefings" / "opportunities sent to me" | MI SaaS core value |

| "Community" / "calls" / "coaches" / "livestreams" | Pro Member retention |
