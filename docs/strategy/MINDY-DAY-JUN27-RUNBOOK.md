# Mindy Day v1 — June 27, 2026 (the reframed launch)

**What it was:** "Mindy Bootcamp / Live Launch" — a training-framed event to the
8,803-person GovCon Giants audience.
**What it is now:** **Mindy Day** — the Elon-style product unveil. Stop teaching the
audience to fish; show the machine catching fish, live. This is the training→tools
pivot made public, and the recording becomes the proof reel that earns the Tier-1
keynotes (PSC Vision, AFCEA) in `MINDY-STAGE-STRATEGY.md`.

**Audience:** ~8,803 bootcamp leads + ~9,900 users (warm, captive). **Date:** Jun 27.
**Decision (Eric, 2026-06-19):** full reframe — rename + live-demo-led + customer hero.

---

## The one rule
**No teaching until after the wow.** A bootcamp opens with an agenda. Mindy Day opens
with Mindy doing something that makes the room lean in. Every minute of "how to" is
earned by a minute of "watch this."

---

## Run of show (5 beats, ~30–40 min)

**1. COLD OPEN — the live find (no intro, no agenda).** (~5 min)
Eric on screen, Mindy already open. "Watch this." Pull up a REAL expiring high-value
vehicle → generate the incumbent/response in ~30s → show the real $ figure. First words
out are a demo, not "welcome to."
*Asset needed: a pre-vetted real expiring vehicle with a big, clean number (data mine).*

**2. THE TURN — "I taught 8,803 of you to do this by hand. Now Mindy does it."** (~3 min)
The explicit pivot. Name the shift. "We scaled the training. This is the tool that does
the work the training taught." Owns the brand evolution out loud.

**3. CUSTOMER HERO — a contractor who WON.** (~7 min)
Real customer, on camera, their win, their dollar figure, their Mindy screenshot. THEY
tell it (Eric interviews, doesn't narrate). This is the "make others money" proof — the
single most credible thing in the whole event.
*Asset needed: one customer with a real, citable win (data mine + Eric outreach).*

**4. THE UNVEIL — one new capability, live.** (~7 min)
Reveal the freshest capability as "the new thing" (candidate: hidden-match — "Mindy
finds the contracts your NAICS codes MISS"). Demo it on real data. This is what makes
Mindy Day a recurring event worth showing up for.

**5. THE CLAIM + THE OFFER.** (~5 min)
Audacious, data-backed: "$X in opportunities surfaced for our users this quarter" (real
number). Then the offer — NOT "sign up for training": **start with Mindy / start a
pilot.** Clear CTA, clear link.

## Risk / honesty (8 days out)
- Customer-hero is the long pole — needs a real person lined up. If none lands in time,
  fall back to a recorded win or a screen-share walk-through of a real user's results
  (still real data, just not live on camera). Do NOT fabricate a testimonial.
- Live demo must use real, pre-checked data (the Market Research accuracy work this week
  means the numbers are now trustworthy on stage).
- Keep the honesty rule from the gov-demo playbook: be explicit about what Mindy doesn't
  do yet. A warm audience forgives edges; they don't forgive overclaiming.
