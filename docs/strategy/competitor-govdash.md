# Competitor brief — GovDash (vs Mindy)

*Researched 2026-06-26 for demo-day positioning. Source: multi-source web research.*

GovDash is the closest thing to a direct Mindy competitor — an AI-native GovCon
platform. This brief: their backstory, what they do well, where Mindy is genuinely
different, and an honest read on our moat (where we're ahead, where we're not).

## Backstory
- **Founded 2023** by Sean Doherty, Timothy Goltser, Curtis Mason.
- **Y Combinator** + VC: Northzone, Mucker Capital, British Columbia Investment Mgmt.
- **~$42M raised**, grew from a handful to **45+ employees**.
- **Traction:** "300+ teams," customers won **$5B+ in awards in 2025**, proposal
  cycles cut to as little as **24 hours**.
- Evolved from a BD tool → self-described **"AI-driven ERP for GovCon"**. Modules:
  **Discover · Capture · Proposal · Contract · Dash** (the AI agent). FedRAMP
  Moderate Equivalency.

## What GovDash does WELL (don't pretend otherwise)
- **End-to-end lifecycle** in one system of record (discover → capture → proposal →
  contract → post-award).
- **Proposal speed at scale** — RFP shredding, compliance mapping, outline→draft,
  24-hour proposals. Mature capture + contract modules.
- **Enterprise-grade** — FedRAMP equivalency, CUI handling, SSO, structured
  onboarding, dedicated support. Built for primes + serious mid-market.
- **Funded + staffed** — $42M and 45+ people means fast product velocity.

## Where MINDY is genuinely different (the moat)
1. **Mindy arrives FULL; GovDash arrives EMPTY.** GovDash's own onboarding requires
   the customer to upload a corpus of past proposals, contracts, RFPs, and resumes
   before it's effective ("the challenge of making LLMs understand everything about
   a company across thousands of documents"). **Mindy brings its own brain on day
   one** — the 604K-record Data Core + a 1,364-doc / 12,534-passage knowledge base
   (8 yrs of teaching, 743 interviews, winning proposals). No data-onboarding tax.
2. **The teaching/coaching moat.** GovDash is a workflow tool. Mindy is built on
   GovCon Giants — 8 years of Eric's teaching + 743 podcast interviews + a real
   audience. Mindy doesn't just DO the work, it TEACHES (coach angle). GovDash has
   **no proprietary education corpus and no audience** to replicate this.
3. **Self-serve + transparent.** GovDash is **sales-gated, quote-based, high-touch
   onboarding** ("submit a form," "less expensive than a lost bid"). Mindy is sign
   up and go. Their own analysis says small contractors "default to more generic,
   lower-cost tools" because of this — **that's exactly Mindy's lane.**
4. **All-in-one AND simple.** GovDash is an enterprise platform needing change
   management + training. Mindy is ask-a-question simple for one person.
5. **Distribution / CAC moat.** GovCon Giants brand + existing community = near-zero
   CAC vs a $42M direct-sales motion. **This is the real durable moat.**

## Where GovDash is AHEAD (be honest)
- **Capital + team + runway** — $42M, 45+ people. We don't match that.
- **Enterprise proposal-automation depth** — mature capture/contract modules,
  24-hr cycles, proven at scale.
- **Security certs** — FedRAMP Moderate Equivalency / CUI. We don't have that yet.
- **Proven enterprise logos** — $5B+ awards, 300+ teams.
- *(Neither is a true back-office ERP — GovDash's "ERP" is procurement-lifecycle,
  no accounting/timekeeping. Unanet owns that lane.)*

## The honest verdict — different segments, not a head-to-head
- **GovDash** = the funded, enterprise, sales-gated platform for contractors who bid
  a LOT, can afford enterprise pricing, and will invest in onboarding their own data.
- **Mindy** = the affordable, self-serve, **day-one-useful**, **teaches-you** brain
  for the ~90% of contractors GovDash can't economically serve — small/individual
  contractors and occasional bidders — built on a **data + education + audience moat
  GovDash can't replicate.**
- We don't win by out-enterprise-ing a $42M company. We win by owning the segment
  they price themselves out of, arriving full instead of empty, and being the coach
  (GovCon Giants) — not just the tool.
