# Customer Interview Script: MI SaaS Validation

**Purpose:** Validate $149/mo MI SaaS model before Laurie call
**Duration:** 10-15 minutes per call
**Goal:** 10 interviews minimum this week

---

## Opening (30 seconds)

> "Hey [NAME], it's Eric from GovCon Giants. Thanks for taking a few minutes. I'm building something new and want to get your honest feedback before I launch it. This isn't a sales call — I just need your brain for 10 minutes. Cool?"

---

## Section 1: Current Usage (2 minutes)

### Q1: What tools are you using?

> "Quick question — what's the ONE tool from your purchase that you use most often?"

**Listen for:** Market Assassin, Alerts, Opportunity Hunter, Briefings, Recompete

**Follow-up:** "How often do you use it? Daily? Weekly? Monthly?"

### Q2: What else are you paying for?

> "Are you paying for any other GovCon intelligence tools right now? GovWin, GovTribe, HigherGov, anything like that?"

**If YES:** "What do you like about it? What do you hate?"
**If NO:** "Have you tried any of them? What stopped you?"

---

## Section 2: The Problem (2 minutes)

### Q3: Biggest BD challenge

> "What's your biggest challenge right now when it comes to finding and winning contracts?"

**Listen for:**
- "Too many opportunities, don't know which to pursue"
- "Don't know who has the contract now"
- "Can't afford expensive tools"
- "Don't have time to research"
- "Need help qualifying opportunities"

### Q4: Time spent on research

> "How much time do you spend each week researching opportunities, agencies, and competitors?"

**Listen for:** Hours per week, frustration level

---

## Section 3: The Solution (3 minutes)

### Q5: Daily briefings value

> "Imagine every morning you got an email that told you:
> - The top 3-5 opportunities you should pursue TODAY
> - Why you can win them (scored by your certifications, NAICS, past performance)
> - Who has the contract now and when it expires
> - What the agency is struggling with (pain points)
>
> Would that save you time?"

**Listen for:** Yes/No, how much time, what's missing

### Q6: Price sensitivity

> "If that daily intelligence cost $149 a month — which is 94% cheaper than Deltek GovWin — would you pay for it?"

**If YES:** "What would make it a no-brainer?"
**If NO:** "What would make it worth $149/mo?"
**If HESITANT:** "What price would feel right?"

### Q7: Team access

> "If your whole BD team — say 3-5 people — could access this for $499 a month, would your company pay for that?"

**Listen for:** Team size, decision maker, budget authority

---

## Section 4: The Gaps (2 minutes)

### Q8: What's missing?

> "What's the ONE thing we could add that would make you say 'I have to have this'?"

**Listen for:**
- Team access
- Custom reports
- Specific agencies
- Competitor alerts
- Teaming partner finder
- Mobile app

### Q9: Deal breakers

> "What would make you NOT buy this, even if the price was right?"

**Listen for:** Complexity, learning curve, no free trial, contract lock-in

---

## Section 5: Conversion (1 minute)

### Q10: Ultimate Bundle upgrade (for OH Pro buyers only)

> "You bought Opportunity Hunter Pro. What's preventing you from upgrading to the Ultimate Bundle with all the tools?"

**Listen for:** Price, don't need it, didn't know about it

### Q11: Referral

> "If this existed today at $149/mo, who else should I talk to? Any other contractors who'd want this?"

**Capture names/emails**

---

## Closing (30 seconds)

> "This was incredibly helpful. I'm launching this in the next few weeks. Would you want early access at a discount — maybe $99/mo for the first 3 months?"

**If YES:** "I'll send you details this week."
**If NO:** "No problem. Thanks for your time."
