## Channel 2: Tuesday Call with Students

### New Format for This Week (Wealth GENIUS Framework)

**Duration:** 20-30 minutes

**Agenda:**

1. **Opening (2 min)**
   > "Today's a little different. Instead of training, I have TWO questions for you. I want to make sure I'm building what you actually need."

2. **Question 1 (10 min)**
   > "What is the ONE thing about GovCon Giants—if I took it away—you would not want to work with us anymore?"

   - Let them answer freely
   - Don't lead them
   - Follow up with: "Tell me more about that..."
   - Write down EXACT words

3. **Question 2 (10 min)**
   > "What is the ONE thing you wish we added that would make you stay forever?"

   - Let them answer freely
   - Follow up with: "What would that look like for you?"
   - Write down EXACT words

4. **Close (3 min)**
   > "Thank you. This is exactly what I needed. I'm building something based on this feedback and you'll be the first to know."
