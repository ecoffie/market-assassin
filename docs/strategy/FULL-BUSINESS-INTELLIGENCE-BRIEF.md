
**Problems:**
- Crowded market (every GovCon coach offers training)
- AI commoditizing course content
- Low completion rates (55% average)
- Race to bottom on price

### New Model (Competing with Deltek)

```
Traffic → Free Tools → MI SaaS ($149/mo) → Enterprise expansion
```

**Advantages:**
- Massive price disruption (97% cheaper than Deltek)
- AI-powered delivery (infinite scale)
- Enterprise customers (higher LTV)
- Training becomes retention, not product
