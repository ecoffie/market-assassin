## Executive Summary

Most small business federal contractors don't have a Business Development team — they ARE the BD team. The owner does sales, delivery, HR, and accounting. They might have a part-time consultant helping occasionally.

**GovCon Giants will give them a BD Department they never had.**

Not replacing humans (Dorsey's model). **Adding capability** they couldn't afford.

---

## The Reframe: Adding, Not Replacing

### Jack Dorsey's Model (Block)

> "Replace what the hierarchy does"

Dorsey cut 4,000 employees to have AI do middle management. This is **subtraction**.

### Our Model (GovCon Giants)

> "Give them a department they never had"

SMBs don't have BD teams to replace. We're not taking away — we're **adding**.

| Dorsey | GovCon Giants |
|--------|---------------|
| Replace existing employees | Add capability to solo operators |
| Subtraction | Addition |
| Controversial | Empowering |
| "AI took my job" | "AI gave me superpowers" |

**Key insight:** The owner of a 5-person 8(a) firm isn't threatened by AI BD tools. They're desperate for them.

## Market Analysis

### Who Doesn't Have a BD Team?

**Federal contractor size distribution:**
- < 10 employees: ~85% of registered small businesses
- 10-50 employees: ~12%
- 50-500 employees: ~2.5%
- 500+: ~0.5%

For the 85% with <10 employees:

- Owner does everything
- Maybe 1 admin/office manager
- Occasional outside consultant ($150-300/hour)

**Annual BD cost if they hired:**
- Entry BD Manager: $80K-$100K salary

- **98% cheaper than hiring**

- If 1% adopt at $199/month: **$3.4M ARR**

## What Does a BD Department Do?

### Traditional BD Team Functions

| Function | Activity | Time/Week |
|----------|----------|-----------|
| **Market Research** | Find opportunities, analyze trends | 10 hrs |
| **Opportunity Qualification** | Score opps, decide pursue/no-pursue | 5 hrs |
| **Pipeline Management** | Track opportunities through stages | 3 hrs |
| **Agency Intelligence** | Research buyers, pain points, history | 8 hrs |
| **Competitive Analysis** | Track competitors, win/loss analysis | 4 hrs |

| **Reporting** | Status updates, forecasts | 2 hrs |

That's a full-time job. The owner of a 5-person firm doesn't have 42 hours/week for BD.

## Product Definition

### The AI BD Department

**Product name:** GovCon BD AI (or "Your AI BD Team")

**Core promise:**
> "We do the 30 hours of research so you can spend 10 hours on relationships."

### The Five BD Agents

Instead of one monolithic product, think of it as 5 AI "team members":

#### 1. Scout (Market Intelligence Agent)

**Role:** Finds opportunities before you do

**Capabilities:**
- Daily SAM.gov, Grants.gov, Forecasts scan
- NAICS-specific opportunity alerts
- "Why this matters to you" context
- Competitor bid tracking

**Output:** Daily/weekly briefing email

**Existing:** This is essentially the current briefing system

---

#### 2. Analyst (Qualification Agent)

**Role:** Tells you which opportunities to pursue

**Capabilities:**
- Win probability scoring (0-100)
- Bid/no-bid recommendation with reasoning
- Competitive landscape assessment
- Effort vs. probability matrix

**Output:** Scored opportunity cards with "Pursue / Watch / Skip" recommendations

**Example output:**
```
📊 BID/NO-BID ANALYSIS

Opportunity: HUD Program Management Support
Agency: Department of Housing & Urban Development
Value: $2.5M - $5M | Closes: April 25 (12 days)

WHY PURSUE:
✅ 8(a) set-aside — you qualify
✅ 541611 exact NAICS match
✅ No incumbent — new requirement
✅ Past performance at HHS aligns
✅ Agency has budget increase (+18% FY26)

⚠️ Short timeline — need to move fast
⚠️ May require CMMC Level 2 (verify)

• Smith Consulting (won similar at VA)
• TechGov Partners (incumbent on adjacent)

• Proposal: 40-60 hours
• Team needed: Yes (need PM past performance)

```

#### 3. Tracker (Pipeline Agent)

**Role:** Keeps your opportunities organized

**Capabilities:**
- Automatic pipeline population from Scout alerts
- Stage progression reminders
- Deadline alerts (7 days, 3 days, 1 day)
- Win/loss tracking with reasons
- Pipeline health scoring

**Output:** Weekly pipeline status, deadline reminders, win/loss analysis

**Integration:** Syncs with existing BD Assist pipeline (if user has it)

### Data Sources

| Agent | Primary Sources | Secondary Sources |
|-------|----------------|-------------------|
| Scout | SAM.gov MCP, Grants.gov MCP, Multisite MCP | USASpending MCP |
| Analyst | Scout output + Agency Forecasts | Win/loss history |
| Tracker | User input + Scout alerts | Email parsing |
| Intel | Budget docs, USASpending, Pain Points DB | Web scraping |
| Partner | Contractor DB, SAM.gov Entities | LinkedIn (manual) |

### AI Model Usage

| Agent | Model | Reason |
|-------|-------|--------|
| Scout | Rules-based + GPT-4o | Structured search, light summarization |
| Analyst | Claude Opus | Complex reasoning for bid/no-bid |
| Tracker | Rules-based | Deterministic state management |
| Intel | Claude Sonnet + RAG | Document analysis, summarization |
| Partner | Rules-based + embeddings | Matching algorithm |

### Database Schema (Additions)

```sql
-- Agent activity log
CREATE TABLE bd_agent_activity (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY,
  user_email TEXT NOT NULL,
  agent_name TEXT NOT NULL, -- scout, analyst, tracker, intel, partner
  activity_type TEXT NOT NULL,
  input_data JSONB,
  output_data JSONB,
  created_at TIMESTAMP DEFAULT NOW()
);

-- Bid/no-bid decisions
CREATE TABLE bid_decisions (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY,
  user_email TEXT NOT NULL,
  opportunity_id TEXT NOT NULL,
  decision TEXT NOT NULL, -- pursue, watch, skip
  score INTEGER,
  reasoning JSONB,
  created_at TIMESTAMP DEFAULT NOW()
);

-- Teaming recommendations
CREATE TABLE teaming_recommendations (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY,
  user_email TEXT NOT NULL,
  opportunity_id TEXT,
  partner_uei TEXT,
  partner_name TEXT,
  match_score INTEGER,
  reasoning TEXT,
  status TEXT DEFAULT 'recommended', -- recommended, contacted, declined, teamed
  created_at TIMESTAMP DEFAULT NOW()
);
```
