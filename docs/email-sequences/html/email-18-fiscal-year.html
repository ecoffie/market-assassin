Quick fiscal calendar lesson:

The federal government's fiscal year ends September 30th. And every year, the same thing happens:

Agencies rush to spend their remaining budget in Q4 (July-September).

Why? Because unspent money often gets reallocated. Program managers who don't spend their budget look like they don't need it.

The numbers:

30-35% of all federal contract dollars spent in Q4
~16% September alone accounts for annual spending
Spike Small business set-aside spending surges as agencies try to meet goals

What this means for you:

If you're positioned with an agency by summer, you're in the running for year-end spending. If you're still trying to get introduced in August, you've missed the window.
