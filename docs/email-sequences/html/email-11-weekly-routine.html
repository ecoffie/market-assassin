Winning government contracts isn't about big bursts of effort. It's about steady, focused action over time.

Here's the 2-hour weekly routine that successful contractors follow:

MONDAY (30 min): Market Scan

  • Check SAM.gov for new opportunities in your NAICS
  • Review Google Alerts for your target agencies (set one up at google.com/alerts)
  • Update your opportunity tracker

WEDNESDAY (45 min): Relationship Building

  • Send one outreach email to an OSBP specialist
  • Follow up on any pending conversations
  • Connect with one new person on LinkedIn

FRIDAY (45 min): Research & Learning

  • Deep dive into one target agency (pain points, org structure, key contacts)
  • Read one article about your agency's mission area
  • Update your capability statement if needed

That's it. 2 hours a week. Consistent effort beats sporadic hustle every time.
