Here's something most contractors don't know about:

8(a) Tribal Entities can receive sole-source contracts up to $100M.

Regular 8(a)

Sole source limited to $4.5M (services) or $7M (manufacturing)

8(a) Tribal

$100M sole source cap

Some of these tribes have won contracts worth hundreds of millions.

Why this matters to you (even if you're not tribal):

These tribal enterprises need partners. They need:

  • Technical expertise they don't have in-house
  • Subcontractors to execute the work
  • Mentor-protégé relationships
  • Joint venture partners

How to find tribal partners (DIY method):

  1. Go to search.certifications.sba.gov
  2. Filter by "Tribal Entity" under ownership type
  3. Enter your NAICS code to find tribes in your industry
  4. Filter by state/region if you want local partners
  5. Review their profiles and reach out directly
