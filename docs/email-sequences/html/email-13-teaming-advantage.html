The Teaming Advantage

Hi {{first_name}},

Let me share something that might change how you think about government contracting:

You don't have to win contracts alone.

In fact, most successful small businesses win their first contracts as subcontractors to larger primes.

Here's why teaming works:

  1. Lower barrier to entry - You don't need past performance on your own
  2. Built-in mentor - Learn how the prime does things
  3. Steady revenue - Cash flow while you build your direct contracting capability
  4. Relationship building - Primes remember good subs when opportunities arise

How to find teaming partners:

Federal Market Assassin has a dedicated "Subcontracting Opportunities" report that shows you:

  • Prime contractors in your space
  • Their small business liaison officers (SBLOs)
  • Contact information
  • Which agencies they work with
  • Whether they have subcontracting plans

It's the fastest way to find primes who need partners like you.

SEE PRIME CONTRACTORS IN YOUR SPACE

No Market Assassin yet? Here's a manual approach:

  1. Go to USAspending.gov
  2. Search contracts in your NAICS
  3. Find the top prime contractors
  4. Google "[Company Name] small business liaison" or check their website

The key is this: don't try to go it alone. The government contracting world rewards collaboration.
