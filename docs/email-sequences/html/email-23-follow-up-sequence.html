Sent an email to an OSBP specialist and heard nothing back?

Don't give up. Government people are BUSY. No response usually means they haven't gotten to it, not that they're ignoring you.

The follow-up sequence that works:

The pattern: Always call first, then follow up with an email. Alternate days of the week and hours of the day for calling - this increases your chances of catching them.

Week 1: 3 Calls (alternating hours) + 1 Email

  • Monday 9am - Call
  • Wednesday 2pm - Call
  • Friday 11am - Call
  • End of week - Send intro email

Week 2: 2 Calls + 1 Email with business update

  • Tuesday 10am - Call
  • Thursday 3pm - Call
  • End of week - Email with update about your business

Ongoing: Company update emails

Stay on their radar with periodic updates about your company:

  • New contract win
  • New hire or team expansion
  • Expanded market or service area
  • Added capability or certification

Key principles:

  • Call first, email second - every single time
  • Only leave 1 voicemail per week - don't fill up their inbox
  • Alternate timing - different days and hours each call
  • Give them a reason - company updates keep you relevant
  • Always be respectful of their time
  • This is a long game - stay persistent

Remember: you're trying to build a relationship, not make a sale. Act accordingly.
