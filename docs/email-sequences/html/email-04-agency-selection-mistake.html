STRATEGY TIP

The Agency Selection Mistake

Hi {{first_name}},

Yesterday I gave you access to Opportunity Hunter. Did you run your first search?

If you did, you probably saw a list of agencies spending money in your NAICS code. Great.

But here's where most contractors go wrong:

They chase EVERY agency on the list.

Big mistake.

Here's why: not every agency that spends money in your space is a good fit for YOUR business. Some agencies:

  • Have procurement processes that take 18+ months
  • Prefer large businesses despite set-aside requirements
  • Are notoriously difficult to work with
  • Have budgets getting cut next fiscal year

What winning contractors do instead:

They focus on 3-5 agencies maximum. They learn everything about those agencies. They build relationships. They position themselves as the obvious choice.

Today's mission:

Go back to your Opportunity Hunter results and pick your TOP 3 agencies based on:

  • Highest spending in your NAICS
  • Set-aside contracts that match your certifications
  • Agencies you've heard of or have some connection to

Write down those 3 agencies. These are your primary targets for the next 6 months.
