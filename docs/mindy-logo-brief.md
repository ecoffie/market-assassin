# Mindy Logo Design Brief

## Who is Mindy?

Mindy is an AI-powered market intelligence assistant for federal contractors. She scans 24,000+ government opportunities daily and delivers personalized briefings. Think of her as a tireless BD analyst that works while you sleep.

**Full name:** Mindy (Market Intelligence, Delivered Daily)
**Tagline:** "The big contractors have armies. You have Mindy."
**URL:** getmindy.ai

## 5 Logo Directions to Explore

### Direction 1: Chat Bubble / Assistant
- Speech bubble with "M" integrated
- Suggests conversation, briefings, communication
- Could have subtle "typing dots" or pulse
- **Reference:** Intercom, Slack, Claude AI

### Direction 2: Radar / Scanner
- Radar sweep or signal rings
- "M" in center
- Suggests: always scanning, finding opportunities
- **Reference:** Radar apps, search/discovery tools

### Direction 3: Briefing / Document
- Stylized document or clipboard
- "M" as header element
- Checkmarks or data visualization hints
- **Reference:** Notion, Coda, document apps

### Direction 4: Abstract Lettermark
- Custom "M" with embedded meaning
- Could incorporate: signal waves, upward motion, connection
- Minimal, works at any size
- **Reference:** Stripe, Linear, Figma, Vercel

### Direction 5: Personified Icon
- Abstract face or avatar (not too human)
- Geometric, friendly, recognizable
- Could work as a "character" for content
- **Reference:** Duolingo (but more professional), Headspace

## What to Avoid

- Generic AI clichés (robot head, brain, circuit board)
- Too corporate/government looking
- Too playful/startup-y
- Overly complex (won't scale down)
- Hard to read in small sizes
- Gradients that don't print well
- Anything that looks like existing GovCon tools (GovWin, etc.)
