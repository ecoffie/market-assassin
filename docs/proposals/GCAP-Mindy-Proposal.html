Mindy is an AI-powered federal market intelligence platform: daily opportunity ranking, agency forecasts, expiring contracts, teaming, a 317K-contractor database, pipeline tracking, and per-client workspaces. Your 8 counselors (12 with the new hires) manage all ~1,000 businesses from one dashboard.

The point isn't more tools. It's capability progression made measurable — moving each business from an opportunity, to an honest read on whether they can win it, to closing the gap — and exporting that progression as the report your funder already expects.

The problem this solves for GCAP

Your value is building capability, not handing out leads. But the work of moving a business from "here's an opportunity" to "here's an honest assessment of your ability to win it, and here's the gap to close" lives across spreadsheets, inboxes, and counselors' heads. And every quarter that progress has to be re-assembled by hand into a report for SBTDC / SBA.

Mindy makes the capability work the system of record, so the reporting is a by-product, not a second job.
