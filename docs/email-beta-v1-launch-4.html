Quick question that separates contractors who win from ones who waste months: before you chase an opportunity, do you know who holds it now?

Most people don't check. They see a solicitation, get excited, and write a proposal against a ghost.

The pros do the opposite. Before they write a word, they pull the incumbent: who has it, how big it really is (the ceiling, not the sticker price), when it expires, and which vehicle you'd need to compete. That tells you if it's worth your time — and if you can actually win it.

That's where capture starts. Not with the proposal. With knowing exactly who you're up against.

Your homework: take one opportunity you're eyeing. Find out who holds it today. If you can't beat their position, your time is better spent elsewhere.
