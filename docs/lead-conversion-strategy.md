# Bootcamp Lead Conversion Strategy

**Goal:** Convert 8,804 bootcamp attendees into paying customers

**Research Sources:** Russell Brunson, Alex Hormozi, Dean Jackson, Frank Kern, Ryan Deiss

---

## The Opportunity

- **8,804 warm leads** from bootcamp signups
- Already enrolled in FREE Market Intelligence (Daily Alerts + Briefings)
- They've invested TIME → high intent signal
- **Industry stat:** 50% of leads buy within 18 months, only 15% in first 90 days

---

## Strategy Framework: 5-Phase Conversion

### Phase 1: Immediate Value (Days 1-7)
**Goal:** Deliver so much value they feel obligated to buy

**Tactics:**
1. ✅ **Auto-enrolled in Market Intel** (already done)
   - Daily Alerts showing real opportunities
   - Daily Briefings with win probability scores
   - Weekly Deep Dive analysis

2. **9-Word Reactivation Email** (Dean Jackson)
   ```
   Subject: {First Name}
   Body: Are you still looking for federal contracts?
   ```
   - Send to anyone who hasn't opened emails in 7 days
   - Simple, personal, gets replies
   - [Source](https://medium.com/@thatdeanjackson/an-amazing-9-word-email-that-revives-dead-leads-d1a1e97e5eea)

3. **Quick Win Content**
   - "Found 3 contracts for you" personalized email
   - Based on their NAICS codes
   - Links to Opportunity Hunter (free tier)

---

### Phase 2: Tripwire Offer (Days 7-14)
**Goal:** Convert from free user to BUYER (any amount)

**Ryan Deiss Tripwire Concept:**
> "The conversion of a prospect to a customer, even for $1, is magical."

**Tripwire Options:**

| Offer | Price | Purpose |
|-------|-------|---------|
| **"GovCon Quick Start" PDF** | $7 | Micro-commitment |
| **Opportunity Hunter Pro Trial** | $1 for 7 days | Tool experience |
| **Contract Review Template Pack** | $17 | Immediate utility |
| **"Win Your First Contract" Mini-Course** | $27 | Education value |

**Email Sequence (3 emails over 5 days):**
1. Day 7: "I put together something for bootcamp attendees..."
2. Day 9: "Only 47 spots left at this price..."
3. Day 11: "Last chance - price goes up tonight"

---

### Phase 3: Core Offer (Days 14-30)
**Goal:** Sell flagship products

**Value Ladder (Russell Brunson):**
```
FREE: Market Intelligence (alerts + briefings)
  ↓
TRIPWIRE: $7-27 quick win
  ↓
CORE: $197-497 tools
  ↓
PREMIUM: $997-1,497 bundles
```

**Core Products to Push:**

| Product | Price | Best For |
|---------|-------|----------|
| Content Reaper | $197 | LinkedIn content |
| Market Assassin Standard | $297 | Market research |
| Recompete Tracker | $397 | Finding recompetes |
| Contractor Database | $497 | Finding partners |
| Market Assassin Premium | $497 | Full analysis |

**Behavioral Dynamic Response (Frank Kern):**
- Track which emails they click
- If they click "recompete" content → send Recompete Tracker offer
- If they click "teaming" content → send Contractor Database offer
- Customize follow-up based on behavior

---

### Phase 4: Bundle Upsell (Days 30-60)
**Goal:** Maximize customer value with bundles

**Bundle Offers:**

| Bundle | Price | Value | Savings |
|--------|-------|-------|---------|
| Starter | $697 | $943 | 26% off |
| Pro Giant | $997 | $1,388 | 28% off |
| Ultimate | $1,497 | $1,788 | 16% off |

**Perfect Webinar Strategy (Russell Brunson):**
- Host monthly "GovCon Strategy Session" webinar
- 60-90 minutes of pure value
- Last 15 minutes: bundle offer with bonuses
- Scarcity: "Only for live attendees"

**Webinar Framework:**
1. **Hook:** "How 3 small businesses won $2.7M in contracts last month"
2. **Story:** Your journey + student success stories
3. **Content:** 3 secrets/strategies (real value)
4. **Offer:** Bundle with bonuses
5. **Close:** Urgency + guarantee

---

### Phase 5: Retention & Referrals (Ongoing)
**Goal:** Recurring revenue + word of mouth

**Subscription Products:**
- Alert Pro: $19/mo
- Briefings Pro: $49/mo (post-beta)
- Federal Help Center: $99/mo

**Referral Program:**
- "Give $50, Get $50" for referrals
- Affiliate program for power users
- Testimonial collection after wins

## Email Sequences to Build

### Sequence 1: Welcome + Value (7 emails, 14 days)
```
Day 0: Welcome to Market Intel (what to expect)
Day 1: Your first Daily Alert is coming tomorrow
Day 2: How to read your Market Intel briefing
Day 4: Quick win - found contracts in your NAICS
Day 7: Tripwire offer introduction
Day 10: Tripwire reminder + social proof
Day 14: Last chance tripwire
```

### Sequence 2: Core Offer (5 emails, 10 days)
```
Day 0: "I noticed you're searching for [NAICS]..."
Day 2: Case study - how [customer] won $X
Day 4: Tool demo video
Day 7: Limited time offer
Day 10: Final reminder
```

### Sequence 3: Bundle Webinar (6 emails)
```
Day -7: Webinar invitation
Day -3: Webinar reminder + what you'll learn
Day -1: Tomorrow! Final spots
Day 0: Going live in 1 hour
Day +1: Replay available (24 hours only)
Day +2: Last chance for bonuses
```

### Sequence 4: Win-Back (Dean Jackson 9-Word)
```
Subject: {First Name}
Body: Are you still looking to win federal contracts?
```
- Send to cold leads (no opens in 30+ days)
- Plain text, no HTML
- Wait for replies, then personal follow-up

## Key Principles

### Alex Hormozi - Rule of 100
> Reach out to 100 people each day, spend 100 minutes creating content each day, or spend $100 on ads each day.

### Russell Brunson - Value Ladder
> Move customers up the ladder with increasing value and price.

### Dean Jackson - 9-Word Email
> Simple, personal emails outperform fancy HTML campaigns.

### Frank Kern - Behavioral Response
> Customize follow-up based on what they click.

### Ryan Deiss - Tripwire
> The goal isn't to profit on the tripwire—it's to convert someone from a visitor into a paying customer.
