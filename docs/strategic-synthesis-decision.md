## Recommendation: Sequential Launch

### Phase 1: Daily Bid Target ($49/mo)
**Timeline:** 4-6 weeks
**Investment:** Low (mostly rebranding existing briefing)

**What it tests:**
- Will SMBs pay $49/mo for curated intel?
- Does "bid target" language resonate?
- Can we convert free briefing users to paid?

**Success metrics:**
- 100 paid subscribers in 90 days
- 20%+ open rate on emails
- 5%+ click-through rate

**Why start here:**
- Already have 927 users getting briefings
- Just need to refine + add paywall
- Proves subscription model before bigger investment

### Phase 2: Bid Intelligence Pro ($149/mo)
**Timeline:** Launch after Phase 1 proves subscription model
**Investment:** Higher (need to build pipeline, teaming, win scoring)

**What it adds:**
- "My Bid Targets" dashboard (pipeline)
- "Can I Win?" scoring
- "Partner Finder" teaming
- Weekly strategy calls (maybe)

**Upsell path:**
- Daily Bid Target ($49) → Bid Intelligence Pro ($149)
- 3x price, 3x value

### Phase 3: Bid Intelligence Team ($299/mo)
**Timeline:** After Phase 2 has 200+ subscribers
**What it adds:**
- Multi-user access
- Team coordination
- Shared pipeline
- Slack/Teams integration

| Bundle | Price | Includes |
|--------|-------|----------|

### How They Work Together

```
Market Assassin → "Is this market worth pursuing?" (quarterly strategic)
         ↓
Daily Bid Target → "What do I bid on TODAY?" (daily tactical)
         ↓
Bid Intelligence Pro → "Track, team, and win" (ongoing execution)
```

**Key insight:** Tools are for deep research (one-time). Subscriptions are for daily execution (recurring).
