Our Market Intelligence tool is now **"Mindy"** — a personified AI assistant.

## Key Messages

### The Problem
> "Every day, $2 billion in federal contracts get awarded. Big contractors have entire teams finding opportunities. Small businesses have spreadsheets."

### The Solution
> "Mindy is the $150K capture manager you can't afford — except she works 24/7 and costs less than your coffee budget."

### The Hook
> "The big contractors have armies. You have Mindy."

### The Proof
> "Mindy scans 24,000+ opportunities, 7,600+ forecasts, and tracks every federal dollar in your NAICS codes — every single day."

---

## What NOT to Do

❌ Don't make Mindy too robotic ("I am an AI assistant...")
❌ Don't make her too cutesy or childish
❌ Don't use generic AI imagery (brains, robots, circuit boards)
❌ Don't oversell — she finds opportunities, doesn't write proposals (yet)
❌ Don't forget the CTA — always "link in bio" or clear next step
