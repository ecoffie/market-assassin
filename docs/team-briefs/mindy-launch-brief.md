## TL;DR

We're rebranding our Market Intelligence platform as **"Mindy"** — a personified AI assistant that finds federal contracts for small businesses.

**What's changing:**
- Name: "Market Intelligence" → "Mindy"
- Voice: Product → Personality (she talks to users)
- Content: Feature-focused → Character-driven

**What's NOT changing:**
- URL moving to `getmindy.ai`
- Pricing stays the same (Free / $149 Pro / $499 Teams)
- Product features stay the same

---

## Why "Mindy"?

### The Problem We're Solving

Our Market Intelligence tool is powerful but invisible. Users say "I use GovCon Giants' tool" not "I use Market Intelligence."

Meanwhile, Cal AI (a calorie tracking app) built by two teenagers hit **$50M ARR** and got **acquired by MyFitnessPal** — largely through TikTok content featuring their AI as a character.

### The Opportunity

We have:
- A working product (24K+ opportunities scanned daily)
- Active users getting daily briefings
- Eric's platform to create content

We're missing:
- A name people remember
- A "character" for social content
- A hook for TikTok/Reels

**Mindy fixes all three.**

## Content Strategy

### The Cal AI Playbook

Cal AI's success came from:
1. **Founder-led content** — Posting daily showing the product
2. **"Demo moment"** — Point phone at food → instant calories
3. **Micro-influencer army** — 150+ creators posting 4x/month
4. **Volume** — 1,000+ total videos

### Mindy's "Demo Moment"

Cal AI had: Point phone at food → calories in 3 seconds

**Mindy's equivalent:**
- Open email → see today's matched opportunities
- "Mindy found 12 contracts while I slept"
- Show dashboard with $ values visible
- "This $2M contract posted 6 hours ago. I'm already prepping."

### Content Formats to Create

| Format | Example |
|--------|---------|
| Demo | "What Mindy found this morning" — screen recording of briefing |
| Comparison | SAM.gov scrolling (frustrated) vs Mindy briefing (easy) |
| Day in life | "How I find contracts with AI" |
| Before/After | "Before Mindy I missed a $400K contract..." |
| Myth buster | "GovCon is only for big companies" (WRONG) |
| Tutorial | "How to read a federal opportunity" |

### Posting Cadence

**Phase 1 (Weeks 1-4):** Eric posts daily
- 7+ videos/week minimum
- Test all formats
- Build the foundation

**Phase 2 (Weeks 5-8):** Add micro-influencers
- Small business creators
- Veteran business owners
- WOSB/MBE advocates
