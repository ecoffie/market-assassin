## What's Happening

We're rebranding our MI tool as **"Mindy"** — an AI assistant character.

**Why?** Two teenagers built a calorie tracking app called "Cal AI", grew it to $50M ARR on TikTok, and got acquired by MyFitnessPal. Their secret? They made the AI a character people could relate to.

We're doing the same thing.

**Mindy** = **M**arket **IN**telligence, **D**elivered dail**Y**

## What's Changing

✅ Voice: Friendly AI assistant personality
✅ Content: Character-driven TikTok/Reels
