# Head of Public Sector Partnerships & Funding

**Company:** GovCon Giants / Mindy ([getmindy.ai](https://getmindy.ai))

**Type:** Full-time
**Reports to:** Eric Coffie, Founder

---

## The opportunity

Mindy is the AI-powered GovCon intelligence platform for small businesses — daily opportunity briefings, 317K-contractor search, market research, proposal assist, and **Coach Mode** for organizations that serve contractors.

We are building **reporting + matching infrastructure** for the coaches who fund federal small business programs:

- **92 APEX Accelerators** (DoD-funded, ~$55M/yr) — must prove client contract wins + **35% Critical Technology Area alignment**
- **1,000+ SBDC offices** (SBA-funded) — must prove capital accessed, jobs created
- **State/local EDCs, workforce boards, MBDA-adjacent orgs** — must prove jobs, capital, and minority business growth in-region

Coach Mode ships. CTA filters ship. NAPEX 2026 (Aug 16–21, Orlando) is the forcing function. **We need one full-time owner** for partnerships *and* non-dilutive funding — not a generic SaaS BD rep who has never read a cooperative agreement.

This role closes org licenses **and** submits SBIR/EDA/state grant applications. You will demo Mindy, talk the CTA mandate without Eric on every call, and turn 60-day director evals into paid contracts.

## What you'll own (~60% partnerships / ~40% funding)

### Partnerships

1. **Center pipeline** — APEX directors, SBDC leads, state/local EDCs, MBDA Business Centers (where operating), NMSDC regional councils, and chamber cohorts. **Recruit and manage** three regional partnership advisors (APEX, SBDC/chamber, minority-business); they open doors — **you close**.
2. **Funder narrative** — Every pitch is **"grant renewal ammunition,"** not "free tool." You speak EDMIS fields, DoD quarterly metrics, and jobs/capital outcomes fluently.
3. **MOU → eval → license** — Track 60-day director evals, NAPEX booth conversions, and cohort deals ($18K–$200K depending on buyer type).
4. **NAPEX 2026** — Pre-book director meetings, run booth conversations, follow up into signed evals within 30 days post-show.
5. **EDC/MBDA landscape research** — Maintain the target list (`PRD-edc-mbda-partnerships.md` Phase 2 backlog); confirm MBDA center funding status before outreach.

### Funding capture

1. **Grant calendar** — SBIR/STTR topics, EDA NOFOs, SBA Growth Accelerator/PRIME, state commerce RFPs.
2. **Application writing** — Draft submissions; Eric and product provide technical input on CTA matching, UEI attribution, and coach APIs.
3. **Dual-track revenue** — SBIR funds R&D build; org licenses fund scale. You keep both pipelines moving.
4. **DoD OSBP / complementary pitches** — National CTA pipeline visibility (complements APEX centers; does not compete for counseling dollars).

- **Closed** at least one B2B or institutional deal ($15K+ ACV) — center license, training contract, or gov-adjacent SaaS
