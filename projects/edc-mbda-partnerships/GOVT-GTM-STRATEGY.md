## The big correction (read this first — it saves months and money)

We started thinking "win an SBIR to fund Mindy." The research says that's the **wrong instrument for our 3 warm contacts** — and the Anduril story shows why.

**SBIR funds R&D you haven't done yet. Mindy already exists and works.** Our three contacts
(Army MICC KO, DISA, Navy OSBP) are **acquisition / small-business people who can simply BUY a
commercial tool** — none of them is an SBIR topic sponsor. Trying to route them through SBIR is
solving a problem we don't have.

**The Anduril lesson (accurately):** Anduril did NOT avoid SBIR — its entire border business runs on
an **SBIR Phase III IDIQ** (FPDS `70B02C20D00000019`, ~$2B ceiling). What they refused was being
**grant-DEPENDENT**. The model:
> Build a real product with your own capital → sell it **fixed-price as a commercial item** to an
> end-user with a budget and real pain → use **SBIR Phase III / OTA as the VEHICLE to scale** a thing
> that already works — not as the reason it exists.

That maps onto us 1:1: Mindy is built; we have 3 end-users with budgets; the fast money is a direct
buy; SBIR's real prize is the **Phase III sole-source door**.

## The two-track strategy

### 🟢 TRACK 1 — SELL DIRECT (now; this is the revenue)

The three warm contacts buy Mindy as a **commercial-item subscription** — fast, no competition, they
can execute it themselves.

| Contact | Need | Buy path (post-Oct-2025 thresholds) |
|---|---|---|
| **Army MICC (KO)** | acquisition market research | **Micro-purchase ≤$15K** pilot (purchase card, no competition) → **Simplified Acquisition ≤$350K** to scale. The KO executes this directly. |
| **DISA** | research expiring contracts | **DITCO** commercial buy (FAR Part 12) or the **Tradewind AI marketplace** (CDAO, DoD-wide). DISA issues no SBIR topics — this was always a direct buy. |
| **Navy OSBP** | SMB market research | **Micro-purchase / SAT** commercial buy + **NavalX Tech Bridge** as the warm channel. |

**Pilot structure (Anduril's CBP arc, scaled down):** land a small **$15K, 60–90 day, single-team
pilot** → deliver → expand to adjacent offices. Land-and-expand, not a moonshot.

**Accelerant:** if Mindy/GovCon Giants holds any **8(a)/SDVOSB/WOSB/HUBZone** cert → **sole-source
set-aside up to $5.5M services**. (Confirm what certs we hold.)

### 🔵 TRACK 2 — ONE SBIR for the PHASE III KEY (strategic, not for income)

Do **one** SBIR — not to get paid to build, but because landing any single SBIR unlocks the
**uncapped, no-recompete Phase III sole-source pathway across EVERY agency** (15 U.S.C. §638(r)(4)).
That's the single most capital-efficient path to real contracts for a small firm — exactly the lever
Anduril used for border towers.

- **Vehicle: AFWERX Open Topic** (the truest tech-agnostic open topic in FY26; Army is narrowing,
  Navy is scoped, DISA has none).
- **Requires a DAF (Air/Space Force) end-user** — our 3 contacts are Army/Navy/Joint, so we need an
  **AF use case + AF customer** (see AF targets below).
- **Reframe the pitch toward a DAF mission** (the dual-use story): not "help vendors win bids" but
  **defense industrial-base mapping / supplier-risk + foreign-influence analytics / acquisition-
  portfolio analytics for COs & PEOs** — which also rides DoD's new foreign-adversary-vetting priority.
- **Precedent that AFWERX funds this category** (the case studies):
  - **Eudia** — $1.25M AFWERX D2P2, AI for DAF **procurement transformation / contract review**
  - **Oliver Wyman** — SSC SBIR Phase III **business-intelligence tool** ($1.1M)
  - **Outside Analytics "DAVE"** — **$215M** AF SBIR Phase III analytics/visualization ecosystem
  - **Certus Core** — AFWERX open topic, pure-software **knowledge-graph datastore**
  - → Mindy sits squarely inside a proven AFWERX funding lane.

## What's transferable from Anduril (honest)

**Copy the sales motion, not the funding model.**

✅ Transfers: sell to one end-user with a budget + painful problem; pilot→expand; position as a
commercial-item subscription (FAR Part 12); lead with a working product + speed; use SBIR as a
*bridge* to Phase III sole-source, not as the business; use free channels (APEX, SBA, DIU/AFWERX,
NavalX) as our "lobbying" substitute.

❌ Doesn't transfer (these are Anduril's starting conditions, not tactics): self-funding speculative
R&D at $1B scale; hardware moat + cinematic demos; paid lobbying from day one; a built-in launch
customer + founder's VC network. **Plan as if we have none of their advantages** — which makes the
direct-pilot path even more important (it's the one we can actually run).
