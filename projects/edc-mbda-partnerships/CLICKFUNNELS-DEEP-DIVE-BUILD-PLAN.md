## 2. LANDING-PAGE MECHANICS (what converts — apply to every Mindy lander)
- **Above-the-fold:** outcome headline **<8 words**, real product screenshot, primary CTA, social proof
  (logos / "1,200+ contractors") — convert without scrolling.
- **Feature→Benefit→Transformation** copy: not "AI opportunity matching" → **"Find the contract you'd
  have missed, before your competitor does."**
- **≤5 form fields** (every extra field costs leads). Mindy free signup = email only ideally.
- **Hook → Story → Offer** structure on every page + ad.
- **Progressive disclosure:** awareness → interest → action down the page; CTA repeated at each stage.

## 3. THE CHALLENGE — "5-Day Find Your First Contract" (gamified onboarding)
Model: One Funnel Away (30-day, daily ~60-min missions, 4 weeks, accountability coach, "test fast/
fail fast"). Mindy's compressed 5-day version:
- **Daily mission email** (unlocks one task/day) + an in-app task → quick win each day.
- Day 1 set up profile/NAICS → Day 2 find your market → Day 3 size up an incumbent → Day 4 pick a
  target → Day 5 draft a response. Each = a Mindy feature, learned by doing.
- **Accountability + community** (Skool or FB group) — the retention + word-of-mouth + content engine.
- Ends with the Pro upgrade (you've shown the value, they've built the habit).
- Gamify: progress bar, streak, "X opportunities found for you", completion badge.
