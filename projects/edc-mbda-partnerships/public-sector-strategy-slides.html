New for us

SBIR, OTAs, and direct sales are open to us. The question isn't whether — it's how, so funding fuels us without owning us. Anduril answered this.

🚀

The smart way (Anduril)

Build the product, sell it fixed-price to an end-user with a budget, use SBIR/OTA only to scale what already works. Funding = fuel.

🪤

The way to avoid

Becoming grant-dependent — getting paid to do R&D on spec. Slow, cost-plus incentives, years before revenue. We skip this entirely.
