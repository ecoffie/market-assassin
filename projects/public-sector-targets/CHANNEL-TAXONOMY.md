## Overview

Every organization belongs to **exactly one channel** and one **`registry_segment`** (`gov_intel`, `nmsdc_council`, `chamber`, `industry_traction`, `minority_operator`, `direct_gov`). See [`MASTER-REGISTRY.csv`](MASTER-REGISTRY.csv) columns `org_level` + `parent_org` for chapter blast radius.

**Commercial models:**

| Model | Channels | Who pays | Member gets |
|-------|----------|----------|-------------|
| **Funder reporting** | APEX, SBDC, EDC, state_division, NMSDC, MBDA_adjacent | Org (grant/co-op budget) | Services via coach; prove to funder |
| **Acquired rights** | trade_501c6, chamber, labor_union, fraternal_veteran, professional_assoc | Association (dues / member benefit budget) | Entitlement — included with membership |
| **Direct government** | direct_dod, direct_af | Agency office | Commercial-item pilot |
