## THE RULE CHANGES (Effective Now)

### FAR Overhaul - What Changed

| What | Status | Impact |
|------|--------|--------|
| **Rule of Two** | SAFE | MUST set aside if 2+ SBs can compete |
| **8(a) Set-Asides** | Now "MAY" | Discretionary, not mandatory |
| **HUBZone/SDVOSB/WOSB** | Now "MAY" | Discretionary, not mandatory |
| **8(a) Follow-on Lock** | REMOVED | Can move to HUBZone, SDVOSB, WOSB |
| **Order-Level Recertification** | GONE | Locked at contract level |

### NDAA FY2026 - Compliance Thresholds Raised

**Cost Accounting Standards (CAS)** - formal accounting systems and audit-ready books:

| Requirement | Old | New | What It Means |
|-------------|-----|-----|---------------|
| CAS Full Coverage | $50M | **$100M** | Only kicks in at $100M+ |
| CAS Applicability | $2.5M | **$35M** | Most SB contracts now exempt |

**Translation:** Bid on contracts up to $35M without expensive CAS-compliant accounting systems.

**Also:** Commercial is now DEFAULT for DoD. Past performance can include commercial contracts.
