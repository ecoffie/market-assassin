Flipping a Requirement: 8(a) → SDVOSB

The FAR 8(a) follow-on lock is GONE — here's how to capitalize

Step 1: Sources Sought Response

  • Monitor SAM.gov for Sources Sought notices
  • Respond even if you won't bid — shapes perception
  • Clearly state your socioeconomic status (SDVOSB, HUBZone, WOSB)
  • Demonstrate capability + past performance

Step 2: Make the Case

  • Reference Rule of Two — 2+ can compete = MUST set aside
  • Show the CO there's competition in YOUR category
  • Request 1-on-1 meeting with buying office
  • Build SBLO relationship — they advocate for you

Key Insight: The contractor who shapes the requirement wins the contract. Don't be passive — go in EARLY.
